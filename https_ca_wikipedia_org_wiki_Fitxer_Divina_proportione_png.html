A rather similar artwork with more gridlines appears on page 70 of De divina proportione (http://issuu.com/s.c.williams-library/docs/de_divina_proportione) and is available at File:Pacioli De Divina Proportione Head Equilateral Triangle 1509.jpg. This drawing appears to have been simplified from there.

Resum

Drawing simplified from a woodcut from the Divina Proportione, Luca Pacioli 1509, Venice, depicting proportions of the human face. The golden ratio does not appear in the drawing.
