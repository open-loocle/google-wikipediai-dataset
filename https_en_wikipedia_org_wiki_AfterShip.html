AfterShip is a Hong Kong startup company[2] offering shipment tracking through SaaS (software as a service) model.[3][4] It was founded after winning the Startup Weekend Hong Kong 2011[5] and Global Startup Battle 2011.[6][7][8][9]

AfterShip received $1 million series A investment from IDG Capital Partners (IDG-Accel) in May 2014.[10][11]

Technology[edit]

AfterShip provides automated shipment tracking as a service,[12] supporting over 600 shipping services worldwide, including UPS, FedEx and DHL.[13][14] AfterShip provides users a dashboard to check the status of shipments across multiple carriers, and sends out emails and notifications automatically at different shipment stages.

AfterShip integrates with major e-commerce platforms like Shopify, Bigcommerce, eBay, Floship and Magento.[13][15]

In Jan 2014, AfterShip launched a package tracking API[16] that allows online retailers to get tracking information from different carriers via AfterShip as a single source.[17] Online retailers can use AfterShip API to display tracking details and information on the website, and monitor carriers’ delivery performance over time.[18]
