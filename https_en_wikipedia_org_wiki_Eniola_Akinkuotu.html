Eniola Akinkuotu is a Nigerian journalist and writer.[1] He studied at the University of Lagos.

Career[edit]

Akinkuotu career as a journalist has spanned different sections of the Nigerian newspaper. Between 2011 and 2014, he works on crime and human rights violation stories, before moving on to the politics desk where he covered stories on the 2014 Ekiti State gubernatorial election.[2]

Akinkuotu has been a journalist with The Punch newspaper since 2011. In 2016, he was working in tandem with the then government policy against corruption. Akinkuotu reportage title was a Runner Up in the Judicial Reporting Category at the Diamond Media Award for Media Excellence.[3] He was the winner of the UNICEF Prize for Child-Friendly Reporting at the 2018 DAME Award.[4] In 2020, he was a Runner Up in the Tourism category of the Nigeria Merit award.[5][6]
