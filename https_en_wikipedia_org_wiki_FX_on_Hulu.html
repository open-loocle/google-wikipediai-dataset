FX on Hulu is an entertainment hub on the streaming platform Hulu which is the home to 40 current, classic, and new series from FX Networks.[1]

FX on Hulu is incorporated into the Hulu streaming service and is offered at no extra cost to the subscribers plan. Most past and current shows produced for FX networks can be found here including exclusive shows produced specifically for FX on Hulu[1]

History[edit]

FX+ an independent streaming service for FX Networks was launched by Fox in September 2018. FX+ operated as a catch up and streaming service for programs on FX and FXX. Following the Acquisition of 21st Century Fox by Disney, Disney acquired the ownership rights to FX Networks and all of its content, as well as a controlling stake in Hulu. Due to a desire by Disney to streamline its streaming services, in March 2020 all FX content was incorporated into Hulu, creating the FX on Hulu brand.[2]

Exclusive content[edit]

FX Networks also produces exclusive content for FX on Hulu that does not air on FX or FXX.[3]

Exceptions[edit]

  • American Crime Story – Due to a 2016 agreement between 20th Century Fox Television (now 20th Television) and Netflix, which predates the Disney acquisition and the launch of FX on Hulu, Netflix holds exclusive global SVOD streaming rights to the ACS franchise (except in Canada), with new instalments arriving on Netflix several months after they finish airing on FX; as such, ACS is not available via FX on Hulu.[13] However, the series is available live and on-demand to Hulu + Live TV customers, as this package includes the FX linear channel.[14]

International distribution[edit]

Hulu is only available in the United States. International availability of FX Networks content varies by region, frequently but not always being distributed internationally by Disney's other streaming services. In parts of Europe and for certain programs in Canada, distribution is through Disney+, specifically its adult orientated Star brand; in Latin America, programming is available on its sister service Star+; and in parts of Southeast Asia and India, these programs are distributed by Disney+ Hotstar.

Due to arrangements that predate the launch of Star in some of these countries, some programming may be carried on third-party channels instead. For example, in Canada, almost all programs originating on the FX and FXX linear channels in the U.S., and certain FX on Hulu programs, premiere exclusively on the licensed Canadian versions of FX and FXX, but some may be made available at a later date on Star. Meanwhile, Mrs. America aired in the United Kingdom on the BBC. FX content is not available on Disney+ Star in Australia due to a licensing agreement with Binge.
