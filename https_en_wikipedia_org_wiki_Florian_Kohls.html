Florian Kohls (born 3 April 1995) is a German footballer who plays as a midfielder for Blau-Weiß 1890 Berlin.

Career[edit]

He made his professional debut on 23 April 2016 in a Bundesliga match against Bayern Munich.[1]

In June 2017, Kohls joined 3. Liga side Würzburger Kickers signing a two-year deal until 2019.[2]
