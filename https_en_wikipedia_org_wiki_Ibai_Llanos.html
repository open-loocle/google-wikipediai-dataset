Ibai Llanos Garatea (born 26 March 1995), commonly known simply as Ibai, is a Spanish internet celebrity, streamer and esports commentator.[1] He is a Twitch and YouTube content creator,[2] and a presenter for G2 Esports until 2021.[3]

Biography[edit]

Ibai was born in Deusto (Bilbao) in 1995.

In 2014, at the age of nineteen, he started his career in League of Legends. In August during Season 7 of LVP Honor Division he narrated his first League of Legends match between wSystem and PainGaming.

In 2016, to be able to work as an announcer he moved from Bilbao to Barcelona.

After broadcasting for the LVP, specifically for the Orange Super League, in February 2019 he started to present a late night show for the Ubeat network called "Hoy no se sale", currently presented by Bruno Feliu and Cristina López, also known as Kapo 013 and Cristinini respectively.

In February 2020, a year after starting this journey, he announced that he was leaving his work at the LVP to become an exclusive content creator for the G2 Esports team, with Ander Cortés, Reven y Ernesto BarbeQ Folch. As of now, he dedicates his entire time to creating content for Twitch.

In his time as an streamer he has carried out several mass events such as "La Liga Santander Challenge" or charity collaborations with La Liga Santander and Banco Santander that took place during the lockdown suffered in Spain in March 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

In January 2021, he announced his departure from G2 and formed his own streaming brand alongside the previously mentioned creators with the addition of Cristinini, Knekro, Werlyb and Illojuan.[4]

On February 5, 2021 he started a new series on his channel called "Chatting Quietly" in which he dedicates to talking (between 30 minutes to 1 hour and a half) with public figures from all branches. In his first program the talk was led by Gerard Piqué who was not the only Football player featured as future shows included Ronaldinho, Paulo Dybala, Sergio Aguero and Sergio Ramos, also interview consisted of artists like Rauw Alejandro, Jhay Cortez, Duki, Nicki Nicole, etc.[5]

Personal life[edit]

Ibai currently lives in Barcelona, in 2021 he separated from G2 and rented a streaming house in which he lives with his 8 friends and various streamers.[6][7][8]

On August 7, in the midst of the uproar over the fate of Lionel Messi's career , the footballer made a farewell dinner in Barcelona and invited Ibai, who attended it together with Coscu and Sergio Agüero. Other celebrities who attended the dinner include Gerard Pique, Shakira, Jordi Alba and many more.[9][10]
