Individual sport

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
A tennis match.

An individual sport is a sport in which participants compete as individuals.[citation needed] However, team competitions in individual sports also occur, such as the Davis Cup and the Fed Cup.
