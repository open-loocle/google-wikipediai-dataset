John Roberts (born 1973/1974) is a British businessman. He is the founder and CEO of AO World, an online retailer specialising in electrical goods.

Early life[edit]

Roberts grew up in Bolton.[2] He went to Bolton School, and earned eight GCSEs, but failed his A-levels in maths, geography and economics.[3][4]

Career[edit]

Roberts started his career as a kitchen salesman.[2]

In 1999 or 2000, he founded Appliances Online, after a friend bet him £1 to set up a business.[4][1]

In May 2019, the Sunday Times Rich List put Roberts' wealth at £210 million.[5]

In February 2017, he stood down as CEO of AO World, and was succeeded by Steve Caunce.[1] Two years later, in January 2019, AO World announced that Caunce was stepping down as CEO, and Roberts was reassuming the role.[6][7]

Personal life[edit]

He is married to Sally Roberts, they have five children, and live in Bolton.[4][2][8] He will not allow his children to work for the company, nor will they inherit, and is "considering putting his wealth into a charitable trust".[2]
