This is a list of devices with Amazon Instant Video UK capability.

Stand-alone products[edit]

Product Manufacturer Device type High definition support Notes
Wii Nintendo Video game console No
Wii U Nintendo Video game console 720p Subtitle support
Xbox 360 Microsoft Video game console 720p Needs Xbox Live Gold Membership at additional cost, subtitle support
Xbox One Microsoft Video game console 1080p Needs Xbox Live Gold Membership at additional cost, subtitle support
PlayStation 3 Sony Video game console 1080p Requires membership of PlayStation Network at no additional cost, subtitle support
PlayStation 4 Sony Video game console 1080p Requires membership of PlayStation Network at no additional cost, subtitle support
Sony BRAVIA/Sony Network Media Player Sony Smart Television/Set-Top Box 720p 2010 and later BRAVIA Internet TV model required. Sony Essentials account required, at no additional cost.
Samsung/LG Smart TVs/Blu-ray players Samsung/LG Smart TV/Internet-connected Blu-ray player 1080p Samsung Smart TV and Blu-ray devices released in 2011, 2012 and 2013 are supported

LG Smart TV and Blu-ray devices released in 2012 and 2013 are supported

Kindle Fire HD Amazon Tablet 720p Pre-installed on all UK models, no separate app needed.
iPad Apple Tablet Amazon Instant Video UK does not hold the rights to stream HD content to iPad
iPhone Apple Phone Amazon Instant Video UK does not hold the rights to stream HD content to iPhone
iPod Touch Apple iPod Amazon Instant Video UK does not hold the rights to stream HD content to iPod Touch
