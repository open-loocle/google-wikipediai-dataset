Netflix is an American global internet streaming on-demand media provider that distributes a number of content called "originals" (films, series, miniseries, etc.) along with acquired content. This article gives a list of originals produced or distributed by Netflix India. Netflix's first original for India was Sacred Games (2018).[1][2]
