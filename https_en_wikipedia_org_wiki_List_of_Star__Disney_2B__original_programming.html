This article is about the content that is found in the Star hub in Canada, Singapore, Europe, Oceania and East Asia inside Disney+. For the content that is found in Star+, the streaming service in Latin America, see List of Star+ original programming.

Star is a hub within the Disney+ streaming service for television and film content intended for a general audience. The hub is available in a subset of countries where Disney+ operates. Programs released exclusively on Star are branded as Star Originals. During The Walt Disney Company Investor Day 2020, it was announced that content from Disney-owned networks such as Hulu and FX would premiere exclusively on Star internationally. It was also announced that Star would produce original local content which will also be exclusively released on the platform.
