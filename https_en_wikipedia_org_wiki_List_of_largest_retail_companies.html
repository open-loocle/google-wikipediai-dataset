This lists rank the largest retail companies according to the Multinational consulting firm Deloitte.

By revenue[edit]

Companies are ordered by revenue from retail operations in millions of US Dollars in FY 2018.[1] Carrefour S.A. was excluded from the 2018's report at the company’s request. The list does not include Wakefern Food Corporation with revenue of US$16.3 billion in 2017.[2]
