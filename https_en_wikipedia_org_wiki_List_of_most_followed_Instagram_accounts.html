Cristiano Ronaldo is the most-followed individual on Instagram, with over 340 million followers.
Kylie Jenner is the third most-followed individual and most-followed woman on Instagram, with over 266 million followers.
Ariana Grande is the fourth most-followed individual and most-followed musician on Instagram, with over 265 million followers.
Selena Gomez was the most followed user on the platform from 2015, until her social media hiatus in late-2018.
Nicki Minaj is the most-followed rapper on Instagram, with over 156 million followers.
Virat Kohli is the most-followed Asian on Instagram, with over 151 million followers.

This article is a list which contains the top 50 accounts with the most followers on the photo and video-sharing social platform Instagram.[1][2][3] As of September 2021[update], the most-followed individual is Portuguese footballer Cristiano Ronaldo with over 340 million followers, and the most-followed female is American reality television star Kylie Jenner with over 266 million followers. Instagram has its own brand account on the platform, and with over 420 million followers; it is the most-followed account overall. National Geographic is the second most-followed brand account, with over 183 million followers. A total of 30 accounts have exceeded 100 million followers on the platform, with nine of those having exceeded 200 million followers.
