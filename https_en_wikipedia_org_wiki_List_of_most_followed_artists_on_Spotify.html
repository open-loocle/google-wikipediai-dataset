List of most-followed artists on Spotify

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Music streaming site Spotify displays data for two types of statistics on the profiles of artists who use its platform: "Listeners" and "Followers". Users can indicate their preference for a particular musician or performer by "following" them. Certain widely-loved acts have attained an exceptionally large number of "followers" on the platform; these acts are listed below.

Ed Sheeran is the most-followed artist on Spotify with over 84 million followers—he was the first artist to cross 50 million followers on the platform.[1] The most-followed female artist is Ariana Grande, with over 68 million followers—she became the most-followed female act on the platform in December 2020 when she surpassed 54 million followers.[2] The most-followed group and non-English act is BTS, with over 38 million followers.[3]
