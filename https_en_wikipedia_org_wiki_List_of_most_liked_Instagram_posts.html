This list contains the top 20 posts with the most likes on the photo and video-sharing social networking service Instagram. The most-liked post on Instagram is a photo of an egg, which has been liked by over 55 million different accounts as of September 2021.[1] The post is also the most-liked internet post of all time of any website.[2][3] The like function has been removed in certain countries, such as Australia, in a trial to reduce the negative effects of "competition" on the platform.[4]

Current record

On January 4, 2019, the account @world_record_egg posted a photo of an egg with the specific purpose of surpassing the then most-liked Instagram post, a picture of Kylie Jenner's daughter with 18.6 million likes.[5] The photo of the egg was originally taken by Serghei Platanov, who then posted it to Shutterstock on June 23, 2015, with the title "eggs isolated on white background."[6] The creator of the @world_record_egg account was Chris Godfrey, an advertising creative, and his friends CJ Brown and Alissa Khan-Whelan.[7][8]

On January 14, 2019, the egg post became the most-liked post on Instagram, to which the egg's account owner wrote "This is madness. What a time to be alive."[9] Kylie Jenner, responded with a video on Instagram of her cracking an egg open with the caption: "Take that little egg."[10][11] The egg's original photographer was surprised by his photo's popularity, writing, "Egg is just an egg."[6]
