The most-liked tweet was posted by the account of American actor Chadwick Boseman, announcing his death in 2020.

This list contains the top 30 tweets with the most likes on the social networking platform Twitter. Twitter does not provide an official list but news and mainstream media make lists.[1][2][3][4] As of August 2021, the most-liked tweet has over 7 million likes and was tweeted from the account of American actor Chadwick Boseman, announcing his death from cancer.[5] Three accounts have more than one of the most-liked tweets in the top 30: South Korean boyband BTS has twenty, while former U.S. president Barack Obama and U.S. president Joe Biden each have two.

Notes[edit]

  1. ^ Tweet announcing the death of American actor Chadwick Boseman. Boseman died after complications with colon cancer, which he had been diagnosed with four years prior; the diagnosis was only made public after his death.[7] It became the most-liked tweet in the history of Twitter within 24 hours of its being posted, and was the most-liked tweet of 2020.[8]
  2. ^ This tweet was the first of three, by former US President Barack Obama, quoting a passage from Nelson Mandela's autobiography, Long Walk to Freedom, in response to the 2017 Charlottesville attack.[10] The accompanying picture, taken in 2011 by former White House photographer Pete Souza, shows Obama visiting a day care center in Bethesda, Maryland.[10] Twitter publicly confirmed four days later, on August 16, 2017, that it had become the new most-liked tweet on the platform ever with over 3.3 million likes at the time of announcement.[10] The tweet surpassed previous record holder Ariana Grande's Manchester tweet on August 15, 2017, when it reached 2.7 million likes three days after being posted.[11]
  3. ^ President-elect Joe Biden posted this tweet on the day of his inauguration as the 46th president of the United States.[13]
  4. ^ Obama wrote this tweet in tribute to American basketball player Kobe Bryant after news of his death in a helicopter crash surfaced.[15] According to Twitter's year-end review, it was the second most-liked tweet of 2020.[16]
  5. ^ American comedian Andy Milonakis posted this tweet in response to the launch of Crew Dragon Demo-2 by SpaceX on May 30, 2020 during the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic and George Floyd protests.[18] According to Twitter's year-end review, it was the third most-liked tweet of 2020.[16]
  6. ^ Vice president-elect Kamala Harris tweeted a video of herself congratulating president-elect Joe Biden over the phone after the pair won the 2020 United States presidential election.[20] According to Twitter's year-end review, it was the fifth most-liked tweet of 2020.[16][21]
  7. ^ BTS member Jungkook posted a selfie featuring his newly-dyed blond hair.[23] The tweet surpassed 1 million likes in 58 minutes.[24]
  8. ^ Joe Biden's first tweet after defeating Donald Trump in the 2020 United States presidential election. It accumulated over 1 million likes in less than half an hour of being posted.[26]
  9. ^ American actor Macaulay Culkin, best known for his role as young Kevin McAllister in the Home Alone and Home Alone 2 movies, posted this tweet a day after his 40th birthday.[28] Intended as a joke to "make people feel old", the tweet elicited thousands of shocked responses from fans, many of whom still thought of Culkin as the film character.[29] According to Twitter's year-end review, it was the fourth most-liked tweet of 2020.[16]
  10. ^ BTS member Jungkook posted a selfie of him wearing a purple sweatshirt (the color is symbolic of the band's fandom) and a black beanie, with the caption "ARMY I miss you" in reference to the band's fans.[31]
  11. ^ BTS member V posted a pair of selfies from behind-the-scenes of a photoshoot.[33] In December 2020, Twitter revealed that his tweet was both the most-liked K-pop related tweet and the most-liked BTS tweet of the year with 3.2 million likes.[34]
  12. ^ Video clip of BTS member Jungkook singing "Never Not" by American singer Lauv.[36] The tweet accumulated 1 million likes within two hours; it was additionally the fastest in 2020 to reach 1 million views.[37]
  13. ^ BTS member Jungkook posted a selfie with self-dyed blue hair.[39]
  14. ^ BTS posted two group selfies of the band celebrating the debut of its single "Butter" at number one on the Billboard Hot 100[41]‚Äîit was the band's third number-one debut and fourth number-one song on the chart overall.[42]
  15. ^ Video clip of BTS member Jungkook singing the BTS remix version of "Savage Love (Laxed ‚Äì Siren Beat)" by New Zealand music producer Jawsh 685 and American singer Jason Derulo.[45]
  16. ^ BTS member V tweeted this video of him wishing fellow bandmate Jungkook a happy birthday in Korean in celebration of Jungkook's 23rd birthday that day.[47]
  17. ^ User Jamie Gagnon of Wisconsin tweeted a video of a sock puppet eating cars, making light of the quarantine imposed during the COVID-19 pandemic. His tweet accumulated over 3 million likes for the period March 16‚Äì22, 2020‚Äîit was the most liked tweet of that week.[49]
  18. ^ BTS tweeted this video of the band celebrating in response to the news that its latest single "Dynamite", released on August 21, 2020, had remained at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 for a second consecutive week.[55]
  19. ^ User Mel tweeted in all caps about beating cancer during the COVID-19 pandemic.
