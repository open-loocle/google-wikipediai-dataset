Justin Bieber is the most-subscribed artist on YouTube Music.
Blackpink is the most-subscribed female act on YouTube Music.

This article lists the thirty most-subscribed artists on the music platform YouTube Music. Artists' subscribers are consolidated from various YouTube channels they may have, including Vevo channels, into Official Artist Channels.[1]
