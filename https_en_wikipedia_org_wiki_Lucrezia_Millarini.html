Lucrezia Millarini (born 16 February 1976) is a British journalist working for ITV News.

Early life[edit]

Millarini was born in a family of Italian descent.[1] Before starting her career in journalism, Millarini studied law at Bristol University and went on to train as a barrister. Initially working for a local radio station in Oxford, Millarini joined ITN's digital operation in London, reporting for the digital 24-hour rolling news channel.[2]

Career[edit]

In December 2010 she joined ITV News London as the Entertainment Correspondent. In January 2013 she was appointed a newsreader. At present, Millarini now regularly presents the main 6pm programme as well as the ITV Lunchtime News (since 2017), ITV Weekend News (since 2015),[3] the ITV Evening News (since 2019) and the ITV News at Ten (since 2021). As well as fronting the news, Millarini has also reported for ITV's flagship current affairs programme, On Assignment.[4]

Millarini reported on the births of two of the Royal Babies and was reporting on the coverage of the Wedding of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle.[5]

She participated on Dancing on Ice on ITV in January 2020.[6]

She won an episode of Celebrity Mastermind broadcast in December 2020.

On 9 April 2021, she broke the news of the death of Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh in a special announcement on ITV.

Personal life[edit]

Millarini lives in South East London[7] with her husband, journalist Simon Kurs, whom she married in 2011, and dog, Milo.[8] Millarini is vegetarian.

Charity[edit]

Millarini is a patron of animal welfare charity Dogs on the Streets.[9]
