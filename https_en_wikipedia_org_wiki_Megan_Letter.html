Megan Elizabeth Letter (born March 17, 1995) is an American YouTuber primarily known for her Roblox videos.[1] Prior to YouTube, Letter studied Graphic Design in college.[2] In mid 2020, Letter and her husband Zack launched a game studio called Wonder Works Studios.[3] Letter also runs an online store, Stay Peachy.[1]

Career[edit]

YouTube[edit]

In 2018, Letter was relatively unknown. At the time, she was making content on The Sims.[1] After switching to Roblox after being unable to pay bills, she rapidly began gaining popularity.[3] As of April 2021, Letter was getting 40 million monthly views and had more than three million subscribers on her channel, MeganPlays.[1] She is most known for making Adopt Me! videos.[4]

Wonder Works Studios[edit]

In 2020, Letter and her husband "Zach," created a Roblox game studio called Wonder Works Studios.[1] One of the reasons is because Letter wanted more control over her content.[5] Wonder Works also chose Roblox over Steam because most of her online followers already have Roblox installed on their device.[5] Their first release was Overlook Bay, where you can adopt a pet, build a house and explore the city.[1][4] Overlook Bay has been played more than 160 million times.[1] The studio has also released another game called Traitor.[3]
