Spotify Singles is a series of original songs and covers by artists, recorded in various Spotify studios and released for streaming only that started in 2016.[1] Spotify pays for recording, mixing and mastering the session.[2] In 2018, Spotify announced that Spotify Singles had accumulated a combined total of 1 billion streams.[3]

Controversy[edit]

Danish pop group Lukas Graham covered the XXXTentacion song "SAD!" for Spotify Singles in 2019. The song was removed less than a week later when the band learned about the deceased rapper's alleged history of abuse.[4][5]
