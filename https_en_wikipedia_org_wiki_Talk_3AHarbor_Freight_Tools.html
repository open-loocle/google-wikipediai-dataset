Update Website Information[edit]

Can someone update the information about their e-commerce division? It seems it no longer exists. Several attempts to visit their web site results in only an advertisement for items recently introduced into their brick and mortar stores. All the links for other parts of their website are dead. Attempts to use their search feature return no results not even a message saying there are no results. The contact link is dead. The "all departments" link is dead. I'm under the impression they are going out of business or at least have shut down the e-commerce division. I wanted to compare some of their tools with other venders but can't do that if the web site returns no results. — Preceding unsigned comment added by 108.190.183.221 (talk) 00:13, 12 July 2019 (UTC)
