Usage

This template should not be used for previous seasons because it is computationally expensive and its sole purpose is to ease updates. Upon the end of the regular season, this template should be fully substituted (perhaps with Special:ExpandTemplates).

conference
Put West or East
divisionxleader
Replace the divisionx's with the names of the divisions in the conference, in lowercase. The numbers should indicate the conference ranking of each division leader.
div#name
The names of the three divisions, in lowercase.
  • abbr – The three-letter (uppercase) abbreviation for the team.
  • HW – Home wins
  • HL – Home losses
  • RW – Road wins
  • RL – Road losses
  • div – W–L record within the division
  • div name – Name of the division
