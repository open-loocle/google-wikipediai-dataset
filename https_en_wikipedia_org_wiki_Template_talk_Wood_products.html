how detailed?[edit]

This template links to Lumber and to a few articles which are made of lumber such as wall stud and purlin. Should we limit these related links to lumber only or also include slat, batten, lath, flitch beam, post (structural), (girt), rafter, sill plate, bressummer, cruck, Stilts (architecture), and maybe others? Jim Derby (talk) 02:47, 16 March 2014 (UTC).

Hi Jim Derby, Good question! I'd say go ahead and add these if they are indeed wood products. Kind regards, DA Sonnenfeld (talk) 13:56, 16 March 2014 (UTC)
