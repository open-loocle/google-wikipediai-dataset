Thomas Simons[4] (born 9 April 2004),[1] better known by his online alias TommyInnit, is an English YouTuber and Twitch streamer. He produces Minecraft-related videos and livestreams, including collaborations with fellow YouTubers and streamers in the Dream SMP,[5][6] which caused his YouTube and Twitch channels to increase in popularity.[7][8] As of 3 September 2021, his seven YouTube channels have collectively reached over 22.4 million subscribers and over 1.69 billion views;[9] his main Twitch channel has reached over 6.4 million followers,[10] making him the most-followed Minecraft channel on Twitch,[11] as well as the 12th most-followed overall.[12][13]

Early life

Simons was born in Nottingham, England, on 9 April 2004.[14]

Career

YouTube

Simons created his first YouTube channel, Channelnutpig, on 15 February 2013[15] and his TommyInnit channel on 24 December 2015.[16] He uploaded his first video on his TommyInnit channel on 9 September 2018.[17] Simons typically uploads videos consisting of him playing Minecraft.

On 6 August 2019, Simons uploaded his first video relating to the Hypixel minigame SkyBlock.[18] While consistently uploading SkyBlock-related content, his TommyInnit channel rapidly increased its subscriber count from 4,800 to 66,000. On 4 July 2020, Simons joined the roleplay-focused Minecraft server Dream SMP,[19][20] run by eponymous YouTuber Dream.[21]

On 1 April 2021, Simons created a new YouTube channel under his own name,[22] only uploading the first video onto it two months later.[23]

On 13 August 2021, Simons starred in the music video for Sub Urban and Bella Poarch's song, "Inferno".[24][25]

Twitch

Simons began streaming on Twitch in late 2018, where he streamed Minecraft, PUBG, and Fortnite.[7][26]

On 20 January 2021, Simons livestreamed the finale of the Dream SMP, titled The Dream SMP Finale, which peaked at over 650,000 viewers,[27] making it the third-highest all-time concurrent viewer livestream on Twitch, overtaking Ninja's Fortnite collaboration with Drake.[28][29][30]
