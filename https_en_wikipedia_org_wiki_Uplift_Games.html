Uplift Games

It has been suggested that this article be merged into Adopt Me!. (Discuss) Proposed since June 2021.

Uplift Games is a remote game studio based in North America and the United Kingdom with 40 employees as of 2021.[1] The studio is best known for being the studio behind Adopt Me!.[2] The company was organized in 2021 to combat confusion, as they previously went by multiple different names, such as "Team Adopt Me" or "Dreamcraft."[3][4] In 2021, Josh Ling, the director of business operations stated that the studio was working on other games besides Adopt Me!.[5] By the end of 2022, Uplift Games is aiming to become the first 100-person studio based on Roblox.[6]

Video games[edit]

Adopt Me[edit]

Main article: Adopt Me!

Adopt Me! is a massively multiplayer online game developed by Uplift Games on the gaming and game development platform Roblox.[7] The original focus of the game was a role-play wherein players pretended to be either a parent adopting a child, or children getting adopted, but as the game was developed further, its focus shifted to being about adopting and caring for a variety of different virtual pets, who can be traded with other players.[8]
