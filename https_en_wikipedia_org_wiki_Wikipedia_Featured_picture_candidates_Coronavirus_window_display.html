Coronavirus rainbow window display[edit]

Voting period is over. Please don't add any new votes. Voting period ends on 22 May 2021 at 12:20:01 (UTC)

Original – The street front of a private house in Walthamstow, London, decorated with a rainbow, bunting, children's drawings and the letters NHS, referring to the role of the National Health Service during the COVID-19 pandemic. Illustrating a cultural trend for window decorations and especially the rainbow symbol to counter negativity during periods of lockdown.
Reason
Good quality image, subject is a cultural phenomenon not well covered in Wikipedia.
