This article is about the Tommy Dorsey album. For other uses, see Yes Indeed (disambiguation).

Yes, Indeed! is an album by Tommy Dorsey and His Orchestra, released in 1990 by Bluebird RCA. This album contains recordings of Tommy Dorsey from 1939 to 1945.[1]
