Zameen.com (Lit: land, Urdu: زمین‎) is an online property portal headquartered in Lahore, Pakistan that lists real estate dealers, developers, agencies, and properties for sale and rent across Pakistan.[1][2]

History[edit]

Zameen.com was founded in 2006 by two Pakistani entrepreneur brothers, Zeeshan and Imran Ali Khan, and in 2015 was brought under the umbrella of the Dubai-based Emerging Markets Property Group, which also owns property portals including Bayut in the UAE, Saudi Arabia and Jordan; Bproperty.com in Bangladesh; Mubawab in Morocco and Tunisia; and Kaidee in Thailand.[3]

In April 2020, EMPG and the OLX Group announced the merger of their operations in Pakistan, Egypt, Lebanon and the UAE,[4][5] as a result of which, the OLX Group became EMPG’s largest single shareholder, with a 39% stake, while EMPG took over OLX platform operations in Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Kuwait, Qatar and Oman, as well as the Dubizzle platform in the UAE.[6]
