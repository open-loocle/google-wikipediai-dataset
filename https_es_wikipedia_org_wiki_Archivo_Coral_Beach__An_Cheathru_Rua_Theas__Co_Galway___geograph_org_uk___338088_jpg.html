English: Coral Beach, An Cheathru Rua Theas, Co Galway Although the small beach is not named on the OSI 1:50000 map the locals refer to this beach as Coral Beach, indeed this beach is not sand but made up of tiny crushed and broken fragments of coral, again according to the locals, it's the only coral beach in Ireland.
