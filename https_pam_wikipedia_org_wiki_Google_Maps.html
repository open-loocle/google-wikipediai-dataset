Ing Google Maps (a kanita maki lagiung Google Local) metung yang simpling web mapping service application ampong tecnologiang ibat king Google, a libri (para king pamangamit a e pang-negocio), a papalakad kareng dakal a serbiciong makabasi kareng mapa, kayabe ne ing website o karinan king Aptas a Google Maps, Google Ride Finder, Google Transit,[1] ampong mapang makakutkut (embedded) kareng third-party website kapamilatan ning Google Maps API.[2] Miyayampang yang mapa da reng dalan, metung a route planner para king pamaglakbeng lalakad, maka-cochi, o makasaken a pampublico, ampong urban business locator para kareng dakal a bangsa king mabilug a yatu. Agpang king metung king kayang talapaglalang, i (Lars Rasmussen), ing Google Maps metung yang "paralan ning pamag-organisa ning informacion king yatu agpang king geografia".[3]

Uling gagamit yang Mercator projection ing Google Maps, e no apakit deng lugal a malapit kareng polo o pole. Metung yang productung kawangis na ing Google Earth, metung a program a magsarili (stand-alone program) para king Microsoft Windows, Mac OS X, Linux, SymbianOS, ampong iPhone OS a mas dakal ampang a magpalto king mabilug a yatu, kayabe na ing labuad o lugal da reng polo.

Serbiciong kawangis

  • Bing Maps – Microsoft's mapping service with road maps and aerial/satellite imagery
    • TerraServer-USA – public domain (older than five years) satellite imagery via Microsoft servers
    • Bing Maps for Enterprise - formerly Microsoft Virtual Earth
  • deCarta - Drill Down Server - web services platform offers AJAX, Flash, iPhone APIs for mapping, geocoding, routing and reverse geocoding services.
  • Géoportail – a French rival offering detailed aerial photographs of French territories
  • MapQuest
  • Multimap.com
  • OpenStreetMap – a royalty free, editable map of the world
  • Ovi Maps – a service offered by Nokia that allows synchronizing with user's mobile phones
  • Pictometry – a birdseye imagery provider which can be integrated into all mapping programs
  • Seat Pagine Gialle – an Italian competitor offering detailed satellite pictures of Italian territories and navigable street level panoramas of Rome (similar to Street View)
  • Terralink International
  • ViaMichelin
  • Yahoo! Maps
