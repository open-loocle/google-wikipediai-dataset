Facebook, Inc. is an American social media an technology company based in Menlo Park, Californie. It wis foondit bi Mark Zuckerberg, alang with fellae Harvard College students an roommates Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz and Chris Hughes, originally as TheFacebook.com—today's Facebook, popular global social netwarkin wabsteid. It is considered ane o the Big Four technology companies alang with Amazon, Apple, an Google. Facebook is ane o the warld's maist valuable companies.

Facebook affers ither products an services. It acquired Instagram, WhatsApp, an Oculus, an independently developit Facebook Messenger, Facebook Watch, an Facebook Portal.
