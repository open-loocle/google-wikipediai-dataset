Google, Inc (NASDAQ: GOOG), is a U.S. public corporation, first estaiblisht as a privately-haudit corporation in 1998, that designed an manisht the Internet Google sairch ingine.[6][7] Google is heidit at the "Googleplex" in Mountain View, Californie an haes ower 4,000 warkers in its employ. Dr. Eric Schmidt, ance CEO o Novell, wis appointit the Chief Executive Officer whan co-foonder Larry Page teuk his retiral. The company's owerview wab page says "Google's mission is tae sort the warld's wittins an mak it handy fur ane an aw".

Oreeginal Inglis - "Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful".
