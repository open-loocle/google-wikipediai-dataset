Twitter is an online social networkin service an microbloggin service that enables its uisers tae send an read text-based messages o up tae 140 chairacters, kent as "tweets".

Twitter wis creatit in Mairch 2006 bi Jack Dorsey an bi Julie, the social networkin steid wis launched. The service rapidly gained warldwide popularity, wi ower 500 million registered uisers as o 2012, generatin ower 340 million tweets daily an haundlin ower 1.6 billion search queries per day.[5][6] Syne its launch, Twitter haes acome ane o the ten maist visitit wabsteids on the Internet, an haes been describit as "the SMS o the Internet."[7] Unregistered uisers can read tweets, while registered uisers can post tweets throu the wabsteid interface, SMS, or a range o apps for mobile devices.[8]

Twitter Inc. is based in San Francisco, wi additional servers an offices in New York Ceety, Boston, an San Antonio.
