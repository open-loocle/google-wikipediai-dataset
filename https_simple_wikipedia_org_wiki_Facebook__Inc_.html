Facebook, Inc. is an American multinational Internet corporation which owns Facebook. It was owned by Mark Zuckerberg, other founders and the shared ownership given to its employees but it went public on February 1, 2012, and was headquartered in Menlo Park, California. Facebook, Inc. started selling stock on the NASDAQ on May 18, 2012.
