AOL Mail Account Setup and Login Process

Among one of the topmost emailing platforms-AOL offers free email services along with unique and important features. Anyone who has the AOL email account login credentials can access the AOL Mail account by visiting the AOL Mail login webpage. You can visit the AOL login webpage using a browser of your computer or mobile or installing the AOL mobile app on your Android device or iPhone. In case you are new at AOL Mail and looking to access the AOL email services and features then you need to create a new account by visiting the AOLmail.com webpage.

On the login.aol.com webpage, you will find an option to create a new account. While creating the AOL email account you will be asked to choose a username and password for your account. This username and password will help you to access the AOL email account. Once after accessing your AOL email account you should change the password of your account to keep it safe and secure. Let’s move forward to discuss the easy and quick ways for the AOL account creation, login, and changing the account password process.

AOL Mail Login Important Features

  • Email attachment limit: 25 MB.

  • Max mailbox size: Unlimited.

  • Supported protocols: POP3, SMTP, IMAP.

  • Spam protection and Virus protection.

  • Spell checking.

  • Supports SSL/HTTPS.

  • You can add other email accounts such as Gmail and Hotmail.

AOL Mail Account Setup Process

Before moving on to the steps, make sure that you have details like name, date of birth, email address, phone number, etc. as you will need them to fill in the account registration form.

  • Using a browser of your device, visit the AOL Mail login page.

  • Find the ‘Create an account ‘option and tap on it.

  • Fill in the registration form by providing the details that are asked.

  • Set a username and password for your account.

  • Now, you need to provide the verification code.

  • After verifying your account, click on the ‘Next’ button.

AOL Mail Account Login Steps

You can log in to your AOL email account using a mobile or computer. In case you are looking to access your AOL Mail login account on your mobile then you need to download and install the AOL mobile app by visiting the application store available on your device.

  • Using a browser, visit the AOL Sign In page.

  • Or install the AOL mobile app by visiting the application store.

  • On the login page, fill in the username and password fields.

  • Check if the entered details are correct.

  • Then, tap on the ‘Sign in’ button.

Way to Change the AOL Email Account Password

Changing the AOL account password and choosing a strong and unique password can help you to protect your account from fraudsters. To change the password of your AOL Mail login account follow the steps that are mentioned below.

  • In your AOL Mail account, visit www.aol.mail.com/login.

  • Now, find and tap on the ‘Change Password’ option.

  • Provide the old password details to authenticate the process.

  • Set a unique and strong password by including alphanumeric characters.

  • To confirm the password re-enter it.

  • Now, to save the new password you need to click on the ‘Submit’ button.

  • When prompted to confirm the password change activity, tap on the ‘Yes’ button.

  • Finally, you have changed the password of your AOL email account.

Conclusion

To complete the account creation process for the AOL Mail account you need to provide the information that is asked on the sign-up page. From creating an AOL email account to changing its password the complete process is easy and quick.
