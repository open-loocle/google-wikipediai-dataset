Amazon Prime Video: free trial, everything you need to know

Updated: Jul. 21, 2021, 9:03 p.m. | Published: Jul. 21, 2021, 3:20 p.m.
By Mallory Dwortz | mdwortz@mlive.com

We’ve compiled a comprehensive guide to Amazon Prime Video, so you can make the best, most informed decision about your streaming needs. Wondering if Prime Video is right for you? Read on to answer your questions, and compare Amazon’s video service to other movie/TV streaming staples.

What is Amazon Prime Video?

Prime Video is Amazon’s streaming service. Offering a vast selection of movies and TV, this massive collection of content is available through your existing Prime membership, or a separate subscription (more information on plans/pricing below). Begin your 30-day free trial here, and start watching.

“Watch popular movies and TV, award-winning Amazon Originals, and live events - all included with a Prime membership. Members can also subscribe to 100+ channels and get special deals to rent or buy new release movies and more.” -Amazon Prime

What does Amazon Prime Video include?

Prime Video’s list of channels goes on and on. From HBO and Paramount+, to live sports, Showtime, and STARZ—this streaming subscription has everything you need to stay entertained long after that 1-month free trial ends. Follow this link to find the complete list of channels.

How much does Amazon Prime Video cost?

There are multiple avenues to Prime Video. Included in the $119/year Amazon Prime membership, it accompanies Amazon’s other signature features—like fast shipping and ad-free streaming. You can enjoy ad-free Prime Video for $12.99/month, or pay only $8.99 to stream with advertisements. This is certainly comparable to other similar streaming services—with Hulu’s plans ranging from $5.99 to $64.99/month, and Netflix costing anywhere from $5.99 to $17.99/month. Shudder and Paramount+ are slightly cheaper, $4.75/month and between $4.99 and $9.99/month, respectively. But don’t forget, a Prime subscription includes premium channels like Paramount+, as well as many choices in the way of horror. They even have a channel section titled “Shudder Featured movies.”

Amazon also offers lower-priced options for qualifying customers. Prime Student nearly cuts the cost in half, allowing those enrolled in a university to enjoy Amazon Prime for only $6.49/month. This is also accompanied by a whopping 6-month trial period. Those receiving government assistance can try Prime for 50% off ($5.99/month); this includes free, convenient delivery, and Prime Video’s award-winning movies and TV. Click here to check your eligibility and get started.

Follow this link to view all of Amazon’s payment plan options.

Is Amazon Prime Video worth the money?

Well, you have a 30-day free trial period to decide, so don’t sweat the cost just yet. I will say though, whenever I can’t find a movie on Netflix, Hulu, and Shudder, I almost always end up renting it on Amazon. It really does seem like they have everything. Offering such a wide array of movies and TV, a Prime Video subscription is your gateway into an extraordinarily large realm of content. It’s great for horror fans, drama enthusiasts, and viewers always craving the hottest new film. Prime video even has sports—and not to mention a long list of family-friendly options. Try it free for 30 days, and see if Amazon Prime Video is right for you.
